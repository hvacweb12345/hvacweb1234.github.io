Virtual Diagnostics for Your HVAC Needs

At Prestige HVAC Services, LLC, we understand the importance of keeping your HVAC system running smoothly. That's why we offer virtual diagnostics â€“ a hassle-free and COVID-friendly option for troubleshooting common HVAC concerns.

What Are Virtual Diagnostics?

Virtual diagnostics allow us to identify and address HVAC issues remotely. Whether you're facing unusual sounds, performance hiccups, or other concerns, our experienced technicians can guide you through a virtual assessment via phone or FaceTime.

How It Works


1. Schedule a Virtual Session:
Reach out to us to schedule a virtual diagnostic session at a time convenient for you.

2. Connect with Our Technician:
Our experienced technician will connect with you via phone or FaceTime to discuss the issues you're experiencing.

3. Guided Troubleshooting:
We'll guide you through simple troubleshooting steps to identify the problem. Our technician will ask questions and provide clear instructions.

4. Swift Solutions:
If the issue is easily resolved, we'll guide you through the necessary steps. If not, we'll discuss the next steps, whether it's scheduling an in-person visit or recommending further action.

Benefits of Virtual Diagnostics


1. Minimize Exposure:
Your safety is our priority. Virtual diagnostics help minimize unnecessary exposure, making it a safe option during these times.

2. Swift Assessment:
Get a quick assessment of your HVAC issues without waiting for an in-person visit.

3. Cost-Effective:
Virtual diagnostics have a flat fee of $75. If an in-person visit is needed, you'll receive a $75 credit, making it a risk-free option.
